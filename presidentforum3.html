第三届全球外国语大学联盟校长论坛在北京外国语大学举行

The Third GAFSU President's Forum was Held at Beijing Foreign Studies University

2021年09月26日 September 26, 2021
GAFSU秘书处 GAFSU Secretariat
1,567

第三届全球外国语大学联盟校长论坛

2021年9月26日，第三届全球外国语大学联盟校长论坛在北京外国语大学成功举行。本次论坛以"疫情下的全球外语教育与跨文化交流"为主题，吸引了来自全球30余所外国语大学的校长和代表通过线上线下相结合的方式参会。

在全球疫情持续蔓延的背景下，本次论坛聚焦于外语教育在构建人类命运共同体中的重要作用，探讨如何通过创新教学模式、深化国际合作，应对疫情带来的挑战，推动全球外语教育的可持续发展。

论坛开幕式上，教育部相关领导、北京外国语大学校长以及联盟轮值主席分别致辞。他们强调，全球外国语大学联盟作为重要的国际教育合作平台，应进一步加强成员之间的交流与合作，共同探索外语教育的新路径、新模式，为促进不同国家和地区的人文交流与文明互鉴做出更大贡献。

本次论坛设有三个平行分论坛，分别围绕"多语种人才培养模式创新"、"数字技术与外语教育融合"以及"区域国别研究与全球治理"等议题展开深入讨论。与会代表分享了各自学校在疫情期间的教学改革经验，探讨了数字技术在外语教学中的应用，以及区域国别研究如何更好地服务于全球治理。

论坛期间，还举行了全球外国语大学联盟学术委员会成立仪式，并发布了《全球外国语大学联盟2021-2025发展规划》。该规划明确了联盟未来五年的发展目标和重点任务，包括加强成员间的学术交流与合作、推动多语种人才培养、深化区域国别研究、促进数字技术与外语教育融合等。

第三届全球外国语大学联盟校长论坛的成功举办，为全球外语教育界提供了一个重要的交流平台，进一步深化了联盟成员之间的合作与友谊。与会代表一致表示，将以本次论坛为契机，继续加强合作，共同应对全球外语教育面临的挑战，为推动全球外语教育的繁荣发展和构建人类命运共同体贡献力量。

The Third GAFSU President's Forum

On September 26, 2021, the Third Global Alliance of Foreign Studies Universities (GAFSU) President's Forum was successfully held at Beijing Foreign Studies University. With the theme of "Global Foreign Language Education and Cross-cultural Exchange Under the Pandemic," the forum attracted presidents and representatives from more than 30 foreign studies universities around the world, participating both online and offline.

Against the backdrop of the ongoing global pandemic, this forum focused on the important role of foreign language education in building a community with a shared future for mankind, exploring how to address the challenges brought by the pandemic and promote the sustainable development of global foreign language education through innovative teaching models and deepened international cooperation.

At the opening ceremony, relevant leaders from the Ministry of Education, the President of Beijing Foreign Studies University, and the rotating chair of the alliance delivered speeches respectively. They emphasized that as an important international educational cooperation platform, GAFSU should further strengthen exchanges and cooperation among members, jointly explore new paths and models for foreign language education, and make greater contributions to promoting cultural exchanges and mutual learning among different countries and regions.

The forum featured three parallel sub-forums, which conducted in-depth discussions on topics such as "Innovation in Multilingual Talent Cultivation Models," "Integration of Digital Technology and Foreign Language Education," and "Regional and Country Studies and Global Governance." Participants shared their respective universities' teaching reform experiences during the pandemic, discussed the application of digital technology in foreign language teaching, and explored how regional and country studies can better serve global governance.

During the forum, the establishment ceremony of the Academic Committee of GAFSU was also held, and the "GAFSU 2021-2025 Development Plan" was released. This plan clearly defines the alliance's development goals and key tasks for the next five years, including strengthening academic exchanges and cooperation among members, promoting multilingual talent cultivation, deepening regional and country studies, and facilitating the integration of digital technology and foreign language education.

The successful holding of the Third GAFSU President's Forum provided an important exchange platform for the global foreign language education community, further deepening cooperation and friendship among alliance members. Participants unanimously stated that they will take this forum as an opportunity to continue strengthening cooperation, jointly address the challenges facing global foreign language education, and contribute to promoting the prosperity and development of global foreign language education and building a community with a shared future for mankind.
