Nature and Purpose

The Alliance is an open, non-profit, non-legal person organization voluntarily formed by universities with characteristics of foreign language teaching and research, as well as country and regional studies. Adhering to the concept of "unity, mutual trust, cooperation and win-win", the Alliance builds an educational cooperation platform with the connotation of "foreign language teaching research, country and regional studies, and cultural exchanges". The purpose of the Alliance is to realize complementary advantages among members, spread languages and cultures of various countries, build bridges of friendship, strengthen country and regional studies, and promote cultural exchanges and mutual learning among different countries and regions.

Functions and Vision

The Alliance will be committed to building educational exchange platforms, strengthening talent cultivation, and promoting scientific research. Through regular Alliance forums and irregular council meetings, it will enhance mutual understanding among Alliance universities and promote the sharing of school-running concepts and development experience. By exploring new talent cultivation models of transnational training and cross-border mobility, it will promote teacher and student mobility among Alliance universities, and strengthen interdisciplinary, multilingual and compound talent cultivation through cooperative education, professional co-construction, and joint training to actively adapt to the urgent demand for high-level international talents in the development of globalization. By strengthening exchanges and cooperation between research institutions and researchers among Alliance universities, it will encourage joint application for international cooperation research projects and conduct joint academic research to enhance the Alliance's level and influence in foreign language teaching research, country and regional studies, and global governance.

Future Development Plan

Utilizing the Double First-Class Platform to plan long-term development of the Global Alliance of Foreign Studies Universities. Specific projects will include the GAFSU Presidents' Forum, GAFSU Discipline Construction and Innovative Development Series Lectures, GAFSU Newsletter Electronic Journal, GAFSU Photography Competition, GAFSU National International Organization Competency Competition, and GAFSU Work Visibility Report to explore new talent cultivation models of transnational training and cross-border mobility, strengthen interdisciplinary, multilingual and compound talent cultivation, and comprehensively enhance BFSU's global influence.
