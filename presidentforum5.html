第五届全球外国语大学联盟校长论坛在浙江外国语学院举行

The Fifth GAFSU President's Forum was Held at Zhejiang International Studies University

2025-10-24 October 24, 2025
GAFSU秘书处 GAFSU Secretariat
3456

第五届全球外国语大学联盟校长论坛

2025年10月24日，第五届全球外国语大学联盟校长论坛在浙江外国语学院成功举行。来自全球多个国家和地区的外国语大学校长、专家学者齐聚杭州，共同探讨"数字化时代外国语大学的转型与发展"。

本次论坛由全球外国语大学联盟主办，浙江外国语学院承办，以"数字化时代外国语大学的转型与发展"为主题，旨在深化全球外国语大学之间的交流与合作，推动外国语大学在数字化时代实现转型发展。

论坛期间，来自中国、美国、英国、俄罗斯、德国、法国、日本、韩国等多个国家的外国语大学校长围绕"数字化时代的语言教育创新"、"人工智能与外语教育"、"跨文化交流的数字化转型"等议题展开了深入研讨。

浙江外国语学院校长在致辞中表示，作为中国东南沿海的外国语大学，浙江外国语学院将充分发挥自身优势，积极推动全球外国语大学之间的交流与合作，为构建人类命运共同体作出积极贡献。

论坛还通过了《全球外国语大学联盟杭州宣言》，呼吁全球外国语大学加强合作，共同推动数字化时代的语言教育创新，促进跨文化交流的数字化转型，为全球语言教育的发展贡献智慧和力量。

本次论坛的成功举办，进一步加强了全球外国语大学之间的联系与合作，为推动数字化时代外国语大学的转型与发展提供了重要平台，也为全球语言教育的发展指明了方向。

The Fifth GAFSU President's Forum

On October 24, 2025, the Fifth Global Alliance of Foreign Studies Universities (GAFSU) President's Forum was successfully held at Zhejiang International Studies University. Presidents and scholars from foreign studies universities around the world gathered in Hangzhou to discuss "Transformation and Development of Foreign Studies Universities in the Digital Age."

Hosted by GAFSU and organized by Zhejiang International Studies University, the forum focused on the theme "Transformation and Development of Foreign Studies Universities in the Digital Age," aiming to deepen exchanges and cooperation among global foreign studies universities and promote their transformative development in the digital age.

During the forum, presidents from foreign studies universities in China, the United States, the United Kingdom, Russia, Germany, France, Japan, South Korea and other countries conducted in-depth discussions on topics such as "Language Education Innovation in the Digital Age," "Artificial Intelligence and Foreign Language Education," and "Digital Transformation of Cross-Cultural Exchange."

In his speech, the President of Zhejiang International Studies University stated that as a foreign studies university in China's southeast coast, Zhejiang International Studies University will give full play to its advantages, actively promote exchanges and cooperation among global foreign studies universities, and make positive contributions to building a community with a shared future for mankind.

The forum also adopted the "Hangzhou Declaration of the Global Alliance of Foreign Studies Universities," calling on global foreign studies universities to strengthen cooperation, jointly promote language education innovation in the digital age, promote the digital transformation of cross-cultural exchanges, and contribute wisdom and strength to the development of global language education.

The successful holding of this forum further strengthened the ties and cooperation among global foreign studies universities, built an important platform for promoting the transformation and development of foreign studies universities in the digital age, and pointed out the direction for the development of global language education.
