重磅！全球区域国别学共同体研讨会会议通知

CCAS Consortium Workshop Notification

2022年12月15日 December 15, 2022
GAFSU秘书处 GAFSU Secretariat
987

全球区域国别学共同体研讨会

2022年12月18日，由全球区域国别学共同体主办、北外区域与全球治理高等研究院承办的“全球区域国别学共同体研讨会”将于线上举办。热忱欢迎各位领导、专家和学者在线参加会议！

全球区域国别学共同体（CCAS）是全球外国语大学联盟（GAFSU）发起的，由来自181个国家、覆盖100多种语言的学者，秉持“增进国际理解，共创美好未来”（Better Understanding, Better World）理念，形成的国际学者学术网络。

为贯彻共同体“增进国际理解，共创美好未来”的理念和“多视角、全覆盖、重原创、促理解”的原则，由全球区域国别学共同体主办、北外区域与全球治理高等研究院承办的“全球区域国别学共同体研讨会”将于2022年12月18日线上举办。此次会议将通过专家主旨发言、点评和问答、研究成果发布等多种形式，推动各国区域国别学学者的交流互鉴，促进区域国别学知识体系的发展，深化全球治理体系改革及相关全球问题的研究。

会议主题：

包容、共享和全球可持续发展

时间：

2022年12月18日09:00-17:00（北京时间）

会议语言：

中文、英语、法语、阿拉伯语

会议主要流程：

开幕—主旨发言—点评和问答—成果发布—主旨发言—点评和问答—闭幕

CCAS Consortium Workshop

On December 18, 2022, the Global Consortium for Country and Area Studies (CCAS) Symposium, hosted by the Global Consortium for Country and Area Studies and organized by the Institute of Area and Global Governance of Beijing Foreign Studies University, will be held online. Leaders, experts, and scholars are warmly invited to participate in the symposium online.

The Global Consortium for Country and Area Studies (CCAS), initiated by the Global Alliance of Foreign Studies Universities (GAFSU), is an international academic network composed of scholars from 181 countries, covering more than 100 languages. Guided by the vision of “Better Understanding, Better World,” the consortium is committed to enhancing international understanding and jointly building a better future.

In order to uphold the consortium’s vision of “Better Understanding, Better World” and its principles of multi-perspectivity, comprehensive coverage, originality, and the promotion of mutual understanding, the Global Consortium for Country and Area Studies Symposium, hosted by CCAS and organized by the Institute of Area and Global Governance of Beijing Foreign Studies University, will be held online on December 18, 2022. Through keynote speeches by experts, discussions and Q&A sessions, and the presentation of research outcomes, the symposium aims to promote exchanges and mutual learning among scholars of country and area studies worldwide, advance the development of the knowledge system of country and area studies, and deepen research on the reform of global governance systems and related global issues.

Theme:

Inclusiveness, Sharing, and Global Sustainable Development

Time:

December 18, 2022, 09:00–17:00 (Beijing Time)

Languages:

Chinese, English, French, Arabic

Main Agenda:

Opening Session – Keynote Speeches – Commentary and Q&A – Research Outcomes Release – Keynote Speeches – Commentary and Q&A – Closing Session
