学校简介

韩国外国语大学（한국외국어대학교，Hankuk University of Foreign Studies），简称“韩国外大”，由韩国政府创办于1954年，是韩国以外语为特色的综合类大学，也是韩国最早的外语教育机构之一，是世界语言学教育领域最顶尖的大学之一，被誉为“亚洲翻译家的摇篮”。学校是“亚洲校园”、国际大学翻译学院联合会、丝绸之路大学联盟、全球外国语大学联盟成员。
韩国外国语大学拥有2个校区、9个研究生院和18个学院，开设45门外语专业，约有17074名本科生、4166名研究生和博士生，设有17个综合学院，共76个单科学系。学校与世界87个国家和地区的522所院校及教育机构建立了姐妹院校合作关系。
韩国外国语大学为学生提供在大韩贸易投资振兴公社、外交部以及海外公共机构实习的机会，并与联合国和平大学合作开设硕士课程。
在国际排名方面，韩国外国语大学在QS现代语言学领域位列全球第28位，连续多年位居QS亚洲大学排行榜外语类高校第一名，在QS亚洲高校无医学类综合大学排名中位列亚洲第八位（韩国国内排名第一），连续十年被评为韩国国际化程度最高的大学。2025年QS世界大学排名位于651—660区间。

About the University

Hankuk University of Foreign Studies (Korean: 한국외국어대학교), abbreviated as HUFS, was founded by the South Korean government in 1954. It is a comprehensive university with a strong focus on foreign languages and is one of the earliest institutions for foreign language education in South Korea. Recognized as one of the world’s leading universities in the field of linguistics, it is often hailed as the "Cradle of Asian Translators." HUFS is a member of the Asian Campus, the Confédération Internationale des Universités Traductrices et Interprètes (CIUTI), the Silk Road University Alliance, and the Global Alliance of Foreign Language Universities.
HUFS has two campuses, nine graduate schools, and eighteen colleges, offering 45 foreign language majors. The university has approximately 17,074 undergraduate students and 4,166 graduate and doctoral students, organized across 17 comprehensive colleges and 76 individual departments. It has established sister-school and cooperative relationships with 522 institutions in 87 countries and regions worldwide.
In international rankings, HUFS is ranked 28th globally in modern linguistics by QS, has consistently held the top position among foreign language universities in the QS Asia University Rankings, and is ranked 8th in Asia among comprehensive Asian universities without medical schools (first in South Korea). It has been recognized as the most internationalized university in South Korea for ten consecutive years. In the 2025 QS World University Rankings, HUFS is placed in the 651–660 range.
