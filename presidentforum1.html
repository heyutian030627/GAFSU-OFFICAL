第一届全球外国语大学联盟校长论坛在北京外国语大学举行

The First GAFSU President's Forum was Held at Beijing Foreign Studies University

2017年05月19日 May 19, 2017
GAFSU秘书处 GAFSU Secretariat
1,234

第一届全球外国语大学联盟校长论坛

2017年5月19日，第一届全球外国语大学联盟校长论坛在北京外国语大学隆重举行。来自全球多个国家和地区的外国语大学校长齐聚一堂，共同探讨外国语大学在全球化背景下的发展战略与合作机遇。

本次论坛以"全球视野下的外国语大学发展与合作"为主题，旨在搭建一个国际化的交流平台，促进全球外国语大学之间的深度合作，共同应对新时代的挑战与机遇。

论坛期间，各位校长围绕"多语种人才培养模式创新"、"跨文化交流与文明互鉴"、"全球治理与区域研究"等议题展开了深入讨论。大家一致认为，在全球化深入发展的今天，外国语大学肩负着培养具有全球视野、跨文化沟通能力和专业素养的复合型人才的重要使命。

北京外国语大学校长在开幕式上致辞表示，全球外国语大学联盟的成立标志着世界外国语高等教育合作进入了一个新的阶段。联盟将致力于推动成员大学之间的资源共享、学术交流和合作办学，共同提升全球外国语高等教育的水平和影响力。

来自欧洲、亚洲、美洲、非洲等地区的外国语大学校长分别介绍了各自学校的发展经验和特色，并就未来合作方向达成了广泛共识。论坛还通过了《全球外国语大学联盟北京宣言》，明确了联盟的发展目标和合作机制。

第一届全球外国语大学联盟校长论坛的成功举办，为全球外国语大学之间的交流与合作奠定了坚实基础。联盟将以此为契机，继续深化成员大学之间的合作，共同推动全球外国语高等教育的繁荣发展，为构建人类命运共同体贡献智慧和力量。

The First GAFSU President's Forum

On May 19, 2017, the First Global Alliance of Foreign Studies Universities (GAFSU) President's Forum was grandly held at Beijing Foreign Studies University. Presidents from foreign studies universities around the world gathered to discuss the development strategies and cooperation opportunities of foreign studies universities in the context of globalization.

With the theme of "Development and Cooperation of Foreign Studies Universities from a Global Perspective," this forum aimed to build an international exchange platform, promote in-depth cooperation among global foreign studies universities, and jointly address the challenges and opportunities of the new era.

During the forum, presidents conducted in-depth discussions on topics such as "Innovation in Multilingual Talent Cultivation Models," "Cross-cultural Exchange and Civilization Mutual Learning," and "Global Governance and Regional Studies." They unanimously agreed that in today's deeply globalized world, foreign studies universities shoulder the important mission of cultivating复合型 talents with global vision, cross-cultural communication skills, and professional literacy.

The President of Beijing Foreign Studies University stated in the opening ceremony that the establishment of GAFSU marks a new stage in the cooperation of foreign language higher education worldwide. The alliance will be committed to promoting resource sharing, academic exchanges, and cooperative education among member universities, jointly enhancing the level and influence of global foreign language higher education.

Presidents from European, Asian, American, African and other regions introduced the development experience and characteristics of their respective universities, and reached broad consensus on future cooperation directions. The forum also adopted the "Beijing Declaration of the Global Alliance of Foreign Studies Universities," clarifying the alliance's development goals and cooperation mechanisms.

The successful holding of the First GAFSU President's Forum laid a solid foundation for exchanges and cooperation among global foreign studies universities. Taking this opportunity, the alliance will continue to deepen cooperation among member universities, jointly promote the prosperity and development of global foreign language higher education, and contribute wisdom and strength to building a community with a shared future for mankind.
