实力刷屏！全球区域国别学共同体研讨会引热议

CCAS Consortium Workshop Raises Attention Worldwide

2022年12月27日 December 27, 2022
GAFSU秘书处 GAFSU Secretariat
1,890

全球区域国别学共同体研讨会

2022年12月18日，由北京外国语大学主办的全球区域国别学共同体（Consortium for Country and Area Studies, CCAS） 2022岁末学术研讨会在线上举行，本次会议的主题为“包容、共享和全球可持续发展”。

本次研讨会受到了国内外各大媒体的广泛关注，Zoom平台全程直播，高峰在线人数4000多人。新华社、中央广播电视总台（CGTN）、CRI国际台、人民网、中国社会科学网、中国网等多家媒体对论坛进行了包含报纸、网站、脸书、推特等多平台多语种报道。

CCAS Consortium Workshop

On December 18, 2022, the 2022 Year-End Academic Symposium of the Global Consortium for Country and Area Studies (Consortium for Country and Area Studies, CCAS), hosted by Beijing Foreign Studies University, was held online. The theme of the symposium was “Inclusiveness, Sharing, and Global Sustainable Development.”

The symposium attracted extensive attention from major media outlets both in China and abroad. It was livestreamed in full on the Zoom platform, with a peak online audience of more than 4,000 viewers. A wide range of media—including Xinhua News Agency, China Global Television Network (CGTN), China Radio International (CRI), People’s Daily Online, China Social Sciences Net, and China.org.cn—covered the forum through newspapers, websites, Facebook, Twitter, and other platforms in multiple languages.

The Conference was hosted by Beijing Foreign Studies University (BFSU), organized by the Academy of Area and Global Governance of BFSU, co-organized by the Institute of Area and Country Studies, Peking University, and supported by the United Nations System in China.
