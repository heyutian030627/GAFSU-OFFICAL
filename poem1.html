The 1st GAFSU GATRICO Tristich Contest Invitation

GATRICO 2022三行诗大赛即日起正式启动！

大赛主旨

鼓励文化交流，促进语言多样性

大赛主题

或许你曾憧憬成为“unique”的存在，又或许你曾畅想拥抱“unlimited”的未来，又或许你曾期望创造“unprecedented”的功业……“

“We unite the world and prefer a world with U, along with your own stories.”请以U开头的字母为线索，发起头脑风暴，大胆进行你的创作吧！——没有什么，能够比将你丰富的哲思在短短的三行诗里表达更富挑战与浪漫气息了。在这个用文学构建的舞台，我们期待着你的精彩作品！

语言

不限 （为便于交流，非中英投稿可提供英文参考译文）

截稿日期

2022年11月15日

获奖名单公布

2022年12月31日

参赛方式

请先填写报名表（见文末“阅读原文”按钮）,并在截稿日期前将表格和参赛作品以附件的形式发送到gafsu@bfsu.edu.cn。

参赛范围

所有GAFSU成员高校的学生均可参赛。每位参赛者只可提交1首三行诗作品且只能获得一个奖项。

基本要求

字数限制：参赛作品应不超过100字

文件格式：.docx格式

文件命名：参赛者全名-三行诗题目-U开头的线索词汇-参赛者所属大学。例如：杨艾米-爱-Unique-北京外国语大学。

请填写英文版或中文版报名表，并在表中阐述U开头的线索词汇。

奖项设置

大赛设有：

特等奖1名

优秀奖2-4名

特别奖3-5名

参与奖5-10名

最佳组织奖3-5名

所有获奖者将获得证书及奖牌，获奖作品将在《全球外国语大学联盟电子期刊》刊登。

The 1st GAFSU Tristich Contest

AIM

To encourage cultural exchanges and promote linguistic diversity

THEME

You may have promised to be a Unique person, embrace the Unlimited future, or craft an Unprecedented career.

"We unite the world and prefer a world with U,along with your own stories."You may use a word that starts with U as a lead to create a tristich. Nothing is more romantic and challenging than expressing your philosophy and idea in one triplet. We look forward to your excellent work.

LANGUAGE

Any language (preferably with a gloss version in English for communication purpose)

SUBMISSION DUE DATE

November 15th, 2022

ANNOUNCEMENT OF WINNERS

December 31st, 2022

HOW TO PARTICIPATE

First fill out the attached form(the "Read more" button at the end of the article), and then send the form(s) and work(s) of the contestant(s) as attachments to gafsu@bfsu.edu.cn before the submission due.

ELIGIBILITY

Students from GAFSU Member Universities. Each contestant can submit only one triplet, and can be awarded only one prize.

BASIC REQUIREMENTS

Each work shall be no more than 100 words.

The file shall be in .docx format.

Format for the file name: contestant's full name-title of the tristich-the U-word-contestant's university. e.g., Amy Young-Love-Unique–BFSU

Please fill out the entry form in English and explain your U-word lead.

PRIZES & PRIVILEGES

For prizes-

Grand Prize: 1 awardee

Award of Excellence: 2-4 awardees

Award of Special Selection: 3-5 awardees

Award of Selection: 5-10 awardees

Award of Selection: 5-10 awardees

Award of Outstanding Co-organizer: 3-5 Member Universities

All winners will receive a certificate and a medal, and their works will be posted in the GAFSU Newsletter.

全球外国语大学联盟 第一届世界大学生三行诗大赛在北外举办 GATRICO 2022 Tristich Contest Held at BFSU

获奖结果 GATRICO 2022——全球外国语大学联盟第一届世界大学生三行诗大赛成功举办，本次大赛以“World with U”为主题，鼓励参赛者以U开头的字母为线索，创作多语三行诗。比赛旨在增进全球外国语大学联盟高校交流，提高学生情感修养，丰富校园文化生活。

WINNERS GATRICO 2022 - The first GAFSU Tristich Contest was successfully held by the Global Alliance of Foreign Studies Universities. With the theme of "World with U", participants were encouraged to compose multilingual triplets using the letters starting with U as clues. The competition aims to deepen communication among universities in the Global Alliance of Foreign Studies Universities, enhance students' cultivation, and enrich their campus life.
