全球外国语大学联盟发展历程

Global Alliance of Foreign Studies Universities - Development History

记录联盟从创立到发展壮大的重要历史时刻，见证全球外国语大学合作共赢的辉煌历程

Recording the important historical moments of the alliance from its establishment to its growth, witnessing the brilliant journey of win-win cooperation among global foreign studies universities

2015年11月
倡议成立 Initiative

全球外国语大学联盟成立倡议

北京外国语大学成立许国璋语言高等研究院并组织召开外国语大学校长论坛，包括日本东京外国语大学、韩国外国语大学、莫斯科国立语言大学、北京第二外国语学院、北京语言大学、大连外国语大学、对外经济贸大学、广东外语外贸大学、国际关系学院、上海对外经贸大学、上海外国语大学、四川外国语大学、天津外国语大学、外交学院、西安外国语大学、浙江外国语学院在内的近20所中外高校代表共同签署《"全球外国语大学联盟"成立倡议书》，倡议成立全球外国语大学联盟（以下简称"联盟"）。

Initiative for the Establishment of GAFSU

Beijing Foreign Studies University established the Xu Guozhang Institute for Advanced Studies in Languages and organized a Forum of Presidents of Foreign Studies Universities. Representatives from nearly 20 Chinese and foreign universities, including Tokyo University of Foreign Studies (Japan), Hankuk University of Foreign Studies (South Korea), Moscow State Linguistic University (Russia), Beijing International Studies University, Beijing Language and Culture University, Dalian University of Foreign Languages, University of International Business and Economics, Guangdong University of Foreign Studies, China Foreign Affairs University, Shanghai University of International Business and Economics, Shanghai International Studies University, Sichuan International Studies University, Tianjin Foreign Studies University, Xi'an International Studies University, and Zhejiang International Studies University, jointly signed the "Initiative for the Establishment of the Global Alliance of Foreign Studies Universities", proposing the establishment of the Global Alliance of Foreign Studies Universities (hereinafter referred to as the "Alliance").

2017年5月19日
正式成立 Official Establishment

全球外国语大学联盟正式成立

全球外国语大学联盟于北京外国语大学正式成立，并同期举行了"首届全球外国语大学联盟校长论坛"。来自15个国家的30所中外高校自愿加入联盟，并作为理事单位选派学者参与学术论坛。

Official Establishment of GAFSU

The Global Alliance of Foreign Studies Universities was officially established at Beijing Foreign Studies University, and the "First Global Alliance of Foreign Studies Universities Presidents' Forum" was held simultaneously. 30 Chinese and foreign universities from 15 countries voluntarily joined the alliance and selected scholars to participate in the academic forum as governing units.

2019年9月25-26日
发展壮大 Growth

第二届全球外国语大学联盟校长论坛

"第二届全球外国语大学联盟校长论坛"在西安举行。来自全球14个国家40多所高校的校领导与专家学者近200人参加了论坛。论坛开幕式上进行了"全球外国语大学联盟新成员加盟签约仪式"。新增院校的加入标志着全球外国语大学联盟进一步发展壮大，开启新的合作篇章。截至目前，联盟高校规模已增至19个国家的35所高校。

The Second GAFSU Presidents' Forum

The "Second Global Alliance of Foreign Studies Universities Presidents' Forum" was held in Xi'an. Nearly 200 university leaders, experts and scholars from more than 40 universities in 14 countries around the world attended the forum. At the opening ceremony of the forum, the "Signing Ceremony for New Members of the Global Alliance of Foreign Studies Universities" was held. The addition of new institutions marks the further development and growth of the Global Alliance of Foreign Studies Universities, opening a new chapter of cooperation. So far, the scale of alliance universities has increased to 35 universities in 19 countries.

2020年3月
学术交流 Academic Exchange

联盟通讯电子期刊创建

联盟理事会秘书处基于《全球外国语大学联盟章程》宗旨及联盟相关合作机制，联合外语教学研究出版社共同发起并创建联盟通讯电子期刊（GAFSU Newsletter），并于2020至2021年间制作发布了两期电子期刊和一期纸质期刊。2022年3月，秘书处正式发布第三期通讯电子期刊征稿启事。

Creation of GAFSU Newsletter

Based on the purpose of the "Charter of the Global Alliance of Foreign Studies Universities" and the alliance's relevant cooperation mechanisms, the Secretariat of the Alliance Council, in conjunction with Foreign Language Teaching and Research Press, jointly initiated and created the alliance's electronic newsletter (GAFSU Newsletter), and produced and published two electronic issues and one print issue between 2020 and 2021. In March 2022, the Secretariat officially released the call for papers for the third issue of the electronic newsletter.

2021年9月26日
全球发展 Global Development

第三届全球外国语大学联盟校长论坛

"第三届全球外国语大学联盟校长论坛"在北外隆重举行。论坛围绕"外国语大学全球发展新理念"主题，以线上线下相结合方式进行,参会的联盟高校总数达到29个，来自全球12个国家。论坛举行了"全球语言教育共同体倡议（北京2021）""全球指数网"和《北京外国语大学全球战略规划（2020-2050）》发布仪式。

The Third GAFSU Presidents' Forum

The "Third Global Alliance of Foreign Studies Universities Presidents' Forum" was grandly held at BFSU. The forum focused on the theme of "New Concepts for Global Development of Foreign Studies Universities" and was conducted in a hybrid online and offline format. A total of 29 alliance universities from 12 countries around the world participated. The forum held the release ceremony of the "Initiative on Global Language Education Community (Beijing 2021)", "Global Index Network", and "Beijing Foreign Studies University Global Strategic Plan (2020-2050)".

2023年9月14日
AI时代 AI Era

第四届全球外国语大学联盟校长论坛

"第四届全球外国语大学联盟校长论坛"在上海举行。论坛以"人工智能时代外语学科与区域国别学的使命"为主题。来自全球13个国家29所大学的校领导与专家学者共百余人参加论坛，为联盟成立以来参会发言人数最多的一次。

The Fourth GAFSU Presidents' Forum

The "Fourth Global Alliance of Foreign Studies Universities Presidents' Forum" was held in Shanghai. The forum themed "The Mission of Foreign Language Disciplines and Regional and Country Studies in the AI Era". More than 100 university leaders and experts from 29 universities in 13 countries around the world participated in the forum, which was the largest number of speakers since the alliance was established.

2025年10月24日
数智时代 Digital Intelligence Era

第五届全球外国语大学联盟校长论坛

"第五届全球外国语大学联盟校长论坛"在杭州举办。主题为"面向未来·数智赋能·融通发展"。来自全球27个国家60余所高校和机构的170余名专家学者参加论坛。论坛上正式发布了《协同推进数智时代语言教育与研究创新发展的倡议》，呼吁联盟院校共同谋划数智赋能教育教学新思路，共同探索全球语言教育新路径，共同打造全球知识创新新高地，共同构建多元主体参与新生态。

The Fifth GAFSU Presidents' Forum

The "Fifth Global Alliance of Foreign Studies Universities Presidents' Forum" was held in Hangzhou. The theme was "Facing the Future · Digital Intelligence Empowerment · Integration and Development". More than 170 experts and scholars from over 60 universities and institutions in 27 countries around the world participated. The forum officially released the "Initiative on Collaborative Promotion of Innovative Development of Language Education and Research in the Digital Intelligence Era", calling on alliance institutions to jointly plan new ideas for digital intelligence empowered education and teaching, jointly explore new paths for global language education, jointly build a new highland for global knowledge innovation, and jointly construct a new ecology of multi subject participation.
