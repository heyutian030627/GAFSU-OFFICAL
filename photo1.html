GAPHO 2022摄影大赛即日起正式启动！

GAPHO 2022 Photography Contest Launches today!

2021年12月31日 December 31, 2021
GAFSU秘书处 GAFSU Secretariat
1,234

GAPHO 第一届摄影大赛邀请函

The 1st GAFSU Photography Contest Invitation

全球外国语大学联盟第一届摄影大赛

大赛主旨

鼓励文化交流，促进语言多样性

大赛主题

多彩的语言文化

（具体要求）拍摄反映多彩的语言和文化的照片

截稿日期

2022年3月31日

获奖名单公布

2022年6月30日

参赛方式

填写报名表，在截止日期前将表格和参赛作品以附件的形式发送到gafsu@bfsu.edu.cn（报名表见文末二维码，请用英文填写）。

参赛范围

所有GAFSU成员大学的学生均可参赛。每位参赛者可提交不超过2张照片，但无论多少照片入选，每人最多只能获得一个奖项。

照片规格

图片像素应大于3400*2400。文件大小应在3M以上，但不应超过10M。 文件名格式：参赛者全名-照片标题-照片拍摄地点-参赛者所属大学。

奖项设置

大赛设有：

特等奖1名

优秀奖2-4名

特别奖3-5名

参与奖5-10名

所有获奖者将获得证书，获奖作品将刊登在《全球外国语大学联盟电子期刊》上，并在北京外国语大学进行线下展出。

The 1st GAFSU Photograph Contest

AIM

To encourage cultural exchanges and promote linguistic diversity

THEME

Diversified Languages and Cultures

(Description) To take photos that reflect colorful languages and cultures

SUBMISSION DUE DATE

March 31st, 2022

ANNOUNCEMENT OF WINNERS

June 30th, 2022

HOW TO PARTICIPATE

First fill out the attached form, and then send the form and works of the contestant(s) as attachments to gafsu@bfsu.edu.cn before the submission due date(Please find the QR code at the bottom of this page, and fill out the form in English).

ELIGIBILITY

Students from GAFSU Member UniversitiesEach contestant can submit no more than 2 photos, and can be awarded only 1 prize, even if both photos are selected.

ANNOUNCEMENT OF WINNERS

June 30th, 2022

PHOTO SPECIFICATION

Images shall be larger than 3400* 2400 pixels; File size shall be over 3 megabytes but less than 10 megabytes. Format for the file name:contestant's full name-title of the photo-where the photo was taken-contestant's university.

PRIZES & PRIVILEGES

For prizes-

Grand Prize: 1 awardee

Award of Excellence: 2-4 awardees

Award of Special Selection: 3-5 awardees

Award of Selection: 5-10 awardees

All winners will receive a certificate, and their works will be posted in the GAFSU Newsletter and exhibited offline in Beijing Foreign Studies University.
