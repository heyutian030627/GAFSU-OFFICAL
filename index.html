促进全球外国语大学之间的交流与合作，推动语言教育和跨文化研究的发展

自成立以来，联盟通过举办学术研讨会、学生交流项目、教师互访等活动，加强了成员大学之间的联系，为培养具有全球视野和跨文化沟通能力的人才做出了积极贡献。

语言教育

跨文化交流

促进不同文化之间的理解与尊重

The Global Alliance of Foreign Studies Universities (GAFSU) is an international academic alliance composed of top foreign studies universities from around the world. The alliance is dedicated to promoting exchanges and cooperation among member universities, and advancing language education, cross-cultural research, and international understanding.

Since its establishment, the alliance has strengthened connections among member universities through academic seminars, student exchange programs, faculty visits, and other activities, making positive contributions to cultivating talents with global perspectives and cross-cultural communication skills.

Global Vision

Connect top foreign studies universities worldwide, promote global educational cooperation

Language Education

Promote language teaching innovation, enhance language learning quality

Cross-Cultural Exchange

Promote understanding and respect among different cultures
